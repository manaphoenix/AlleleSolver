Punnet Calculator

About

This is a punnett square solver / generator.
Things to keep in mind:

  • a punnett square is a method for visually showing the chance of a trait happening when two allele pairs are crossed.
  • Uppercase letters are dominant and if present always win. this means BB or Bb the dominant "B" trait still wins.
  • Lowercase letters are recessive and only win if a dominant trait is not present.
To begin using this put an allele pair into Pairs A and Pairs B boxes. An allele is always made of up two letters, and can be chained together with multiple different letters. A two chain allele pair is called a dihybrid cross as an example. (IE. BBRR is a dihybrid)
